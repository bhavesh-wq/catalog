CONNECTORS

The EZ-FLO connection is a proprietary and patented component that is critical to the injection process. Proper selection and installation of the connection is essential for generating successful and repeatable injection results. The connection can be sized to match the diameter of the main line or water source and some EZ-FLO connections may also have a reduction in diameter or valve for feeding at lower flow rates. These diameter reductions or valves, when installed and used properly, will not affect the irrigation system's pressure or flow.

Detailed Connector instructions and videos are available on the company's website to help ensure the right product is selected for trouble-free use for years to come.
