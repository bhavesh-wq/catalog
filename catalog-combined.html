EZ-FLO: Innovative

Injection...

EZ-FLO was founded by Dan Gilmore and started as a seedling in his mind while he was fertilizing his yard. Tired of spending too much time on watering and fertilizing his greenery, Dan wanted to create something that could take care of his lawn by itself. This led to the birth of a simple, automatic, proportional injection system that saves time and effort.

Over the years, EZ-FLO has grown from a garage project to a leader in injection equipment, serving professional, retail, and international markets. Today, EZ-FLO is proud to be adopted by everyone, from homeowner associations to whole cities, and is still driven by its founder's passion for making life easier for garden enthusiasts.

SIZING THE TANK

To keep the process simple, we follow a straight forward sizing method that focuses on the irrigated area only for a 4 to 8 week refill period. This ensures you will have ample capacity for a wide range of products.

Quick Sizing:
One Pound of Water Soluble Fertilizer Per 1000 SQFT of Landscape (Irrigated area)

Example:
75,000 SQFT Landscape (50,000 SQFT of Turf & 25,000 SQFT of Trees & Shrubs)
1 pound of capacity per 1,000 SQFT = 75,000 / 1,000 = 75 LBS of Capacity Needed

CONNECTORS

The EZ-FLO connection is a proprietary and patented component that is critical to the injection process. Proper selection and installation of the connection is essential for generating successful and repeatable injection results. The connection can be sized to match the diameter of the main line or water source and some EZ-FLO connections may also have a reduction in diameter or valve for feeding at lower flow rates. These diameter reductions or valves, when installed and used properly, will not affect the irrigation system's pressure or flow.

Detailed Connector instructions and videos are available on the company's website to help ensure the right product is selected for trouble-free use for years to come.
