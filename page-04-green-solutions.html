EZ-FLO Injection

The EZ-FLO Injection system is an innovative solution designed to make the process of fertilizing landscapes faster, easier, and more efficient. With the EZ-FLO system, you can treat a six acre park or a home landscape in minutes versus hours, making it a highly efficient solution for large and small landscapes alike.

By delivering fertilizer directly through the irrigation system, EZ-FLO eliminates the need for manual spreading and reduces the amount of water and labor required. With its ability to deliver the right amount of fertilizer to your plants, EZ-FLO ensures consistent and accurate coverage for optimal plant growth.

Works with all types of irrigation: Rotor, Spray, Drip, & Many More
